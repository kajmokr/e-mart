How do I register an account? Answer added by Johnnie Kang Today 2:40 pm - 24.06.2014
Robert Nowak

Voting

To register, go to the registration page, and follow these steps:
1. Enter your information.
You'll be asked to enter your name and email address, and to choose a password. If you're attempting to buy, sell, or contact a member, we may also ask you to provide contact information. When you click Submit, your account will be created.
2. Review your confirmation.
You'll see a confirmation message with your email address and an eBay username we've created for you. If you would like to change or correct any of your information, just go to My eBay, click the Account tab and go to Personal Information. You're all set! We may need to ask you for a few more pieces of information as you start to buy, sell, or contact another member.

How do I bid on auction items? Answer added by Johnnie Kang Today 7:220 pm - 13.06.2014
Robert Nowak
Auction Buying Help
Voting
11

Once you've signed up for e-mart, you can bid real-time, participating in the auction as it happens. Go to the product list on e-mart. You'll be redirected to a bidding page that will show your bid and the current high bid. If you've been outbid, and the auction is still going, you'll have the option to enter a higher bid up to your bidding limit. The bidding increases (or increments) are determined by the auction house.

I Have Received an Unfair Negative Rating, What Can I Do? Answer added by Johnnie Kang Today 1:30 am - 1.06.2014
Natalie Cole
Translations CC
Voting

The ratings system promotes freedom of speech on e-mart. Buyers and sellers are given the opportunity to respond to any ratings giving their side of the story for all to see. Normally we do not get involved in rating disputes. We encourage users to rate other users with respect to the experience they have while transacting. If you feel that you have received an unfair rating or you have evidence of any abuse of the rating system, please contact us.
